we provide free food and toiletries to the underprivileged communities in Johannesburg, South Africa

MEALS

Monthly feeding drives at underprivileged communities Breakfast packs (includes cereal, sugar, milk, juice) – our aim is for kids to have a healthy breakfast in the morning. These packs are provided on a weekly basis. Lunch packs (bread/flour, peanut butter, jam) – to ensure kids have a sandwich daily. Packs to be provided on a weekly basis. Adhoc daily meals (sandwiches provided to those in need) – we often find kids and adults coming to our door and we would like to ensure that this is always available for them.

TOILETRIES

Basic toiletries: soap, toothpaste, toothbrush, wash cloth, body lotion, roll on, girls sanitary towels. we provide these packs on a monthly basis
