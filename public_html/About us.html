HISTORY

Our organization was established in 1953 to to enrich the lives of children from severely underprivileged communities in Johannesburg, South Africa. This additional funding and recognition led to a growing body of professional NGOs. The Government of South Africa decentralized development activities throughout the 1950s.

OUR MISSION

our mission is to enrich the lives of children from severely underprivileged communities in Johannesburg, South Africa.our aim is to enrich the lives of severely underprivileged children and families in underdeveloped communities. We believe that the necessities we aim to provide is a basic human right.

VISION

the foundations objective is to work in collaboration with other Foundations that mainly deal with providing children and families with the basic human necessities.the foundations aim is to provide kids with the tools that will help them reach their potential. Giving them the necessary advice that will help them grow into the responsible adults they are meant to be. the foundation would like to provide elementary workshops at our monthly drives to the kids. This can only be achieved once the Covid situation has eased and we are comfortably able to reach out to the kids easily.
